Online Marketing

We are looking for a colleague for a newly created position in our marketing department in Ikast. You may need to be educated Multimedia designer with primary interest in social media. You become part of a marketing team and you will get a good opportunity to develop professionally. You have a lot of influence on the job and you will contribute to your input to increase IBF visibility.

We expect you:

  • Has an interest and flair for social media and can manage posts / ads on Facebook, LinkedIn and Instagram
  • Can work with social media advertising goals
  • Know how SEO optimization and AdWords work
  • Can work with CMS systems
  • Has the flair of image processing and interest in video editing
  • Is innovative, has a sense of detail and works independently
  • Is positive, outgoing and self-driving.
primary work assignments:
  • Managing posts / ads in Facebook ad administrator, including follow-up of results and budgets
  • Strengthening our social media efforts. You become the bearer and executive for implementation and implementation
  • Maintenance and development of content on various websites
  • Continuous optimization of SEO
  • Various marketing tasks like Google Analytics data processing and other ad hoc tasks.

It is important that you have a great courage and want to work in a small team where we support and back each other up in the daily work.
