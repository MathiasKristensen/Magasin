Multimedia Designer

The job Are you looking for new challenges and wanting to become part of an international team? Fent Aps in Aalborg is looking for an experienced employee who is trained in web design, graphics or multimedia design. You will be responsible for the preparation of graphic material in the form of campaigns and websites.

Fent is a consulting company with 12 years of experience in the industry. We focus on developing and maintaining e-business solutions and online marketing in collaboration with our customers and partners spread across the globe.

We have an informal working environment where we work independently with our tasks, but where collaboration with for example programmers too important role. You must enjoy responsibility under a free framework, taking responsibility for your tasks and working hours. You will be the third of our web designers and the fifth person at our office in Aalborg, as well as part of a larger international collaboration.

We are a workplace that has well-being and retention at the top of our agenda. Our current designers have been in the company 5 years or more.

The job requires versatility and you have the courage to solve many different tasks in a company, where two days are not the same.

As a person are you:

  • Creative and enterprising
  • Independent, responsible and quick to get you into new tasks
  • Structured, thorough and good to cooperate
  • Have a good eye for web design and ease of use
  • Has technical understanding and aesthetic sense
You should be well-behaved with independent work tasks, but at the same time be conversational and able to work together and help where needed. In addition, you would like to have at least 1-2 years of experience from similar jobs, or acquire the necessary skills in another way.

It is a requirement that you have previously worked on the following:
  • Graphics for web
  • HTML and CSS
  • Must be able to edit in HTML / CSS templates Make designs on your own
  • Have experience with website maintenance (both code and graphics)
  • We expect you to write and speak English on a high level as we have international cooperation
  • Skilled user in Photoshop and Text editor of your choice (Sublime text, Brackets, etc.)
It is an advantage if you have experience with the following:
  • Create a simple landing page on your own
  • Bootstrap and / or other frameworks
  • Any experience with JavaScript

Working hours and wages:

It is a full-time position and we expect to hold conversations on a regular basis.
Startup as soon as possible. It is expected that you can work 37 hours a week. Our normal working hours are Monday-Thursday 9.00-17.00 and Friday 9.00-16.30. Working hours are otherwise flexible and are planned in cooperation with the company. Salary depends on your qualifications and experience.
